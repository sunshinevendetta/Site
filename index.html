information

Started in 2017, releasing music in Rave Toolz from Ducky and Haka Is Dead from T5umut5umu, Sunshine began to gain some online focus, among the new wave of producers focuses into raw new rave music, following their first releases, another labels such as Non Stop Pop started to publish some NXC music from the artist.

 

Also released on two compilations from Weird NXC a net label empowering the NXC culture worldwide, where he often helps also into the development of the compilations helping Gasoiid.

 

Starting 2020 a new phase begin with the Hyperbass Records a label focused into a new variety of music, genres and artists, that have been producing some different radio shows that have really interesting residences at different radio stations worldwide such as Hong Kong Community Radio from Hong Kong, Internet Public Radio from Mexico and others, where we host and invite producers from around the globe to do guest mixes in every new episode.

 

AEON is the debut album from the artist that will be released at the end of the year 2020.
